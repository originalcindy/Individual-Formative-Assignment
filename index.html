Age Equality

Age equality is the idea that all people should be treated equally, regardless of their age, and have access to the same rights and opportunities. The Equality Act 2010 in the UK protects people from age discrimination in a number of ways.

Ways in which age inequality occur

Direct discrimination When someone is treated worse than another person in a similar situation because of their age Indirect discrimination When an organization has a policy or way of working that puts people of a certain age group at a disadvantage Harassment When someone makes you feel humiliated, offended, or degraded.
